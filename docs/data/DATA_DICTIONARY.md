# Data Dictionary

## BBE core metrics

### Demand Power

Modeled measure of brand-driven choice strength. Use as an indicator of demand potential from equity. It is not actual volume share.

### Perceived Value

User-facing label for the Pricing Power source metric. Modeled measure of broad equity-driven value perception and premium/value defense. Do not use for SKU-level price-point, pack architecture, promo-depth, or channel pricing decisions.

### Meaningful

Personal relevance and delivery on category needs.

### Different

Perceived uniqueness, leadership, and trend-setting.

### Salient

How readily the brand comes to mind in relevant consumption occasions.

Preferred input order for user-facing interpretation is Salient, Meaningful, Different. Source files may still use Kantar-style metric naming/order.

## Benchmark views

### Vs Category

Index versus category average, where 100 is the category norm.

### Ahead

Whether the brand is in the top third versus brands in the same size/life-stage peer set.

### Momentum

Statistically significant movement versus prior period.

## Demographic diagnostic evidence

### Official demographic cut

Measured BBE performance data split by an approved demographic segment such as age cohort or gender. A usable official cut should include metric value, benchmark basis, base size, readable-base status, significance or movement status where available, period, source, and owner approval.

### Simulated demographic cut

Prototype-only scenario data used to demonstrate how demographic diagnostics would work once official BBE cuts are loaded. It is not measured consumer evidence and must be labeled as simulated wherever it appears.

### Demographic context

External audience or market composition data, such as Census or other approved demographic sources. It can support audience sizing and context, but it cannot diagnose brand equity performance for that demographic without BBE cut data.

## BrandZ Typology

BrandZ typology describes the brand’s equity shape and typical marketing challenge. It is period/category/market-specific and should not be treated as permanent brand DNA.

## Growth Navigator bridge

Used where available to interpret whether equity is converting through:
- Proposition
- Reach
- Resonance
- Available & Visible
- Delivering Value
- Quality Growth Metrics
