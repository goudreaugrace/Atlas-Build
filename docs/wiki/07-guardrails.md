# Guardrails

Guardrails are part of the product, not legal fine print. They should be visible wherever the user might over-read the data.

## Perceived Value

Perceived Value is broad brand-equity value perception. It is not SKU-level pricing guidance.

Do not use Perceived Value to recommend:

- SKU price changes.
- Pack architecture.
- Promo depth.
- Retailer-specific price moves.

Those decisions require RGM, pricing science, and commercial evidence outside the BBE packet.

## Category Lens

The active category lens defines the benchmark context. A brand may be read inside Snacks or Better-for-You Snacks, but that does not prove substitution or portfolio migration.

## No Causality Claims

The system can identify patterns and diagnostic fit. It cannot prove causality without causal evidence.

## No Cannibalization Or Substitution Proof

The current packet does not prove cannibalization, occasion-level substitution, or consumer migration across brands.

## No One-Wave Overreach

Do not call a brand permanently miscast based on one wave or one metric. Momentum gaps should be called out as missing or directional.

## AI Guardrail

AI can summarize, translate, challenge, and draft. It cannot invent unsupported facts, metrics, treatments, or conclusions.
