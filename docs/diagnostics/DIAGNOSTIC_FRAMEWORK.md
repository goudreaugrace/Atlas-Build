# Diagnostic Framework

## Concept

The system acts like a brand doctor.

It should not merely show metrics. It should interpret the pattern of metrics, explain the diagnosis, and surface treatment paths.

## Diagnostic sequence

```text
1. Confirm category lens
2. Read BrandZ typology
3. Read Demand Power and Perceived Value
4. Read Salient / Meaningful / Different
5. Read benchmarks: Vs Category, Ahead, Momentum
6. Check Growth Navigator bridge if available
7. Generate diagnosis candidates
8. Rank primary and secondary diagnoses
9. Assemble evidence and counter-evidence
10. Match treatment options
```

## Core diagnosis states

### Strong but Slipping

The brand is still powerful but losing momentum in relevance or demand. Treat as maintenance and renewal, not crisis.

### Salient but Generic

The brand comes to mind but lacks a strong reason to choose. Treat distinctiveness before adding more reach.

### Meaningful but Invisible

The brand is relevant to those who know it, but not mentally available enough. Treat salience and occasion memory.

### Different but Niche

The brand has a distinctive edge but lacks broad relevance or salience. Treat translation and selective scale.

### Underbuilt Equity

The brand lacks enough Salient / Meaningful / Different foundation to support a scaled growth role. Treat foundations first.

### Foundation Deficit

The brand is weak across most metrics. Do not over-prescribe advanced tactics.

### Price-Value Vulnerability

The brand may lack enough equity-based value perception. Treat premium/value cues and refer to RGM for SKU decisions.

### Healthy / Monitor

No major diagnosis fires; monitor context and maintain assets.

## Confidence logic

High confidence requires:
- multiple aligned metrics
- benchmark consistency
- typology support
- limited counter-evidence

Medium confidence:
- two strong aligned signals, some missing diagnostics

Low confidence:
- one signal or conflicting data

## LLM role

LLMs do not invent diagnoses. They explain the diagnosis and help the user interrogate it.
